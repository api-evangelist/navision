<h4 class="wp-block-heading" id="a-new-way-to-bring-service-workflows-insights-and-actions-directly-into-copilot"><strong>A new way to bring service workflows, insights, and actions directly into Copilot</strong></h4>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Microsoft 365 Copilot is becoming the primary interface for how people get work done. As more teams rely on Microsoft 365 Copilot to retrieve information, reason over data, and take action, the need for <strong>domain</strong>‑<strong>specific intelligence</strong>, especially for customer service, has never been greater.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">On March 9<sup>th</sup>, we <a href="https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/dynamics-365/blog/business-leader/2026/03/09/a-new-way-of-working-is-taking-shape-frontier-transformation/?msockid=399b4ed5b760617b1e7e5d51b36067ec">announced the frontier transformation</a>, where we introduced a new type of business application integrated with Microsoft 365 Copilot. Today, we’re excited to introduce <strong>Service Agent in Microsoft 365 Copilot</strong>: a purpose‑built agent that brings customer service context, insights, and actions directly into the Copilot experience employees already use every day.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Service Agent enables service teams to move faster, stay focused, and resolve issues with greater confidence, without switching tools or losing context.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading" id="what-is-service-agent"><strong>What is Service Agent?</strong></h4>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Service Agent</strong> is a declarative agent that runs inside <a href="https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/copilot/microsoft-365/microsoft-365-copilot-overview"><strong>Microsoft 365 Copilot</strong></a>, designed specifically for customer service scenarios.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">It combines:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li class="wp-block-list-item">The <strong>reach and familiarity of Microsoft 365 Copilot</strong></li>



<li class="wp-block-list-item">The <strong>depth of Dynamics 365 Customer Service data</strong></li>



<li class="wp-block-list-item">The <strong>power of agents that can reason, retrieve, and take action</strong></li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">With Service Agent, service professionals can interact with cases, knowledge, and service workflows using natural language—grounded in both Microsoft 365 and Dynamics 365 Customer Service system data—right from Copilot.</p>



<h5 class="wp-block-heading" id="why-this-matters-for-it-and-service-leaders"><strong>Why this matters for IT and service leaders</strong></h5>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">For years, service professionals have had to juggle multiple tools: CRM systems, knowledge bases, emails, internal chats, and reports, often switching context.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Service Agent changes that model by making <strong>Copilot the primary system of engagement</strong> for service work.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This approach delivers three key benefits:</p>



<p class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-paragraph"><strong>1. One Copilot experience, across all applications including within Dynamics 365 Customer Service</strong></p>



<p class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-paragraph">Service Agent brings service workflows into the same Copilot surface used for everyday productivity, reducing friction, training overhead, and context switching.</p>



<p class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-paragraph"><strong>2. Faster resolution through richer context</strong></p>



<p class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-paragraph">By grounding Copilot in both <strong>Microsoft 365 data (Outlook, Teams, SharePoint)</strong> and <strong>Dynamics 365</strong> <strong>service data </strong>(such as cases, emails, knowledge, customer history), service professionals can build case understanding in seconds—not minutes.</p>



<p class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-paragraph"><strong>3. Action, not just answers</strong></p>



<p class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-paragraph">Service Agent doesn’t stop at reading and synthesizing data. It can help service professionals <strong>prioritize cases, update records, draft responses to customers, and trigger workflows</strong>—all through natural language.</p>



<h5 class="wp-block-heading" id="what-service-agent-can-do-in-public-preview"><strong>What Service Agent can do in Public Preview</strong></h5>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">In its initial release, Service Agent enables scenarios such as:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-list-item"><strong>Case understanding and summarization</strong><br />Quickly generate rich summaries of customer cases, including context from prior interactions and related knowledge.</li>



<li class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-list-item"><strong>Case prioritization and workload awareness</strong><br />Ask Copilot what needs attention now, based on customer signals and service data.</li>



<li class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-list-item"><strong>Service knowledge retrieval</strong><br />Get relevant answers grounded in Dataverse and SharePoint knowledge, directly within Copilot.</li>



<li class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-list-item"><strong>Make data updates and initiate workflows</strong><br />Make updates to service records, add case notes and initiate workflows such as child case creation without leaving Copilot.</li>



<li class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-list-item"><strong>Cross</strong>‑<strong>app continuity, shared history, shared memory</strong><br />Move seamlessly between applications such as Teams, Outlook, and Dynamics 365 Customer Service while maintaining shared memory and chat history.</li>
</ul>

<h5 class="wp-block-heading" id="built-for-enterprise-requirements"><strong>Built for enterprise requirements</strong></h5>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Service Agent is designed with enterprise IT needs in mind:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-list-item"><strong>Grounded in Microsoft 365 Copilot</strong> with enterprise‑grade security and compliance</li>



<li class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-list-item"><strong>Aligned with existing Dynamics 365 Customer Service investments</strong></li>



<li class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-list-item"><strong>Extensible</strong>, Service Agent will extend to supporting additional skills, apps, and workflows over time</li>



<li class="has-body-medium-font-size wp-block-list-item"><strong>Admin‑friendly</strong>, building on familiar Copilot and Dynamics management models</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Service Agent acts as an intelligent layer on top of existing service systems, bringing the right information and actions to users, when and where they need them across the application ecosystem.</p>
